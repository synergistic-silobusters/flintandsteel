A strategy that enables collaboration and development by providing visibility to innovative ideas.

Innovate

Create an innovative idea and post it to Rok-Starter. This idea will be visible to other innovators around the world.

Collaborate

Browse through posted innovations and give support to ones that are viable. Share ideas with others who may be interested in supporting it. Post comments to an innovator to help mold their vision.

Achieve

Sharing innovations allows all departments to reap the benefits of a single idea. Together we can grow our ideas to make a better company.
